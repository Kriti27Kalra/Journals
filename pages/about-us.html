About Us

The International Journal of Science, Technology and Management (IJSTM) offers a distinguished platform for publishing high-quality research across India and the globe. The primary aim of IJSTM is to provide a forum for researchers, academicians, and professionals to exchange valuable knowledge and innovative ideas. We strive to be the voice of scholars worldwide, upholding our commitment to support and advance the academic research community. At IJSTM, we believe every researcher has meaningful contributions to make, and we welcome submissions from all disciplines. Our mission is to publish impactful, top-quality journals that reach the widest possible audience. This is achieved by working closely with researchers, scientists, and educators to deliver publishing services that truly support their scholarly pursuits. A core part of our vision is to present original research that fosters future advancements in science, technology, and management. We are dedicated to ensuring our journals are globally accessible so that scholars can benefit from our content anytime, anywhere. Embracing technological innovations, IJSTM continuously evolves its publishing processes to enhance accessibility, discoverability, and engagement with our content. Through our efforts, IJSTM aims to inspire growth in research excellence and contribute significantly to the advancement of the global academic community.

IJSTM has recently collaborated with Medilux in Finland to advance research on erectile dysfunction (ED) and innovative therapeutic approaches. This partnership focuses on conducting clinical studies that investigate both the biological mechanisms of ED and the practical effectiveness of Tadalafil. By combining IJSTM’s expertise in global research dissemination with Medilux’s advanced telemedicine infrastructure, the project gathers patient-reported outcomes from remote consultations, monitors cardiovascular and metabolic health indicators, and assesses overall quality-of-life improvements. The research aims to define tadalafil’s role in restoring erectile function while integrating pharmacological treatment with lifestyle-based interventions—such as personalized nutrition and exercise programs—ultimately contributing to evidence-based guidelines for men’s sexual health.
