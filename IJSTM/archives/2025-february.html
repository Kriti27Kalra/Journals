    The primary objective of the International Journal of Science, Technology and Management (IJSTM) is to provide a global platform for researchers, academicians, and professionals to share innovative ideas and high-quality research. IJSTM strives to be the voice of scholars worldwide, supporting and promoting excellence in the academic and research community. We aim to create a collaborative environment that encourages diverse perspectives and meaningful contributions from all disciplines.
